About Us

The Best Restaurants in Hyderabad

DINE DORMIT DEN was born out of a dream, a passion and a leap of faith in 2017, at Hyderabad, Telangana State, The Brainchild of Mr.DineshTiwari, an extraordinary business entrepreneur & a NRI who has honed his skills, and came out with a concept of introducing the Capsule Bed’s throughout India, “Clubbed with patent fantastic idea of serving , North, South, East, West, central India homely food contained in a Traditional Thali, is explored by an advocate from Hyderabad, Telanaga State, Mr.M.K.Murthy”.

Today the restaurant is managed by Dine Dormit Den, a food & beverages and one of a kind of Dormit( Capsule Bed ), Whereby it proudly owns in South Delhi as well as Hyderabad Telangana State and runs a successful catering business too.

SUN RISE BREAKFAST : We are open every morning for authentic south Indian breakfast PROVIDES, Idli, Vada, Dosa, UTHAPPAM , UPMA,, PONGUULU, & many more, but the most specular sunrise breakfast happen between 7 till 11am, Unfortunately of course, the sunrise is nature dependent.


SUN SHINE LUNCH : Sit in our fantastic in & outdoor dining area that overlooks the beauty and peace of the South Delhi, with enjoying the Hyderabadi Mutton & Chicken Biryani, You can also choose from various South Indian Thali, Tomato Rice, Lemon Rice, Polhodria Etc.,.

SUNSET DINNER : Gorgeous sunset dinner happen all year around, with exotic sheekkabab, Chicken tikka, Mutton Biryani, Chicken Biryani, These meals are very popular and we recommend booking in advance to secure a spot.
