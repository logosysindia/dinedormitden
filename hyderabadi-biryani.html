Hyderabadi Biryani

Hyderabadi Chicken Biryani

Hyderabadi Dum Chicken Biryani is world famous dish. This non-vegetarian delicacy is an authentic Hyderabadi dish, rice preparation which is a meal in itself.

Coupled with flavors of spices delicately combined with rice and chicken makes an aromatic combination that is hard to resist. This rice dish has two major ingredients – chicken and basmati rice. The rest is playing around with ingredients and spice mix to create this flavorful Chicken Biryani.
