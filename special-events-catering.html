Special Events & Catering

The Best Restaurants in Hyderabad

We love hosting parties for small and large groups, and can provide catering services for special events, corporate dinners and lunches, and other special events.

We can recommend a menu for a party or special event for groups of 20 to 200 people, providing a flexible service to meet your requirements and budget. Because our management and head chefs are all South Indian natives, we understand exactly your requirements for Non Vegetarian and vegetarian food.

We have provided meals and catering for many visiting VIPs and Indian political figures, as well as large companies and tour groups. You can trust Dine Dormit Den to do everything necessary for a memorable event

How we organize it for you

Contact us to organize your party or special event. ( Link )

Business event : Celebrate the success of an event, and/or use our space for a presentation or training session. Buffet service or set menu, free-flow soft-drinks. Incase if you require : a projector with screen, 'U'-shaped seating arrangements we will try our best to provide you.

Festival celebration : Celebrate Holi or Diwali with a special meal at Dine Dormit Den. We can provide a themed decoration, and can work with you to provide special food items outside of our regular menu.

Tour group : Meals for groups of 20–200 people at our restaurants or other locations— in Delhi or your hotel included! Special menus for Jains and vegetarians include dhoklas and kachoris, as well as exotic desserts like jalebis, gulab jamun, and angoor rabdi. Choose from a menu of over 50 curries and 20 starters.

Home catering : We bring the food to you with home catering. We cook all the food you'll need at our restaurant and bring it to your place, kept warm throughout in our special buffet trays. We can cater for groups of 10–200 people, can arrange music, and can even bring a Tandoor oven for hot and fresh naan bread.

Birthday party for your child : Special kids' menu provided. We can also arrange clowns, face-painting, games, and a themed decoration.
